FAQ

How do i request a transcript?

You can request a transcript by logging into the app, navigating to the transcript section, and selecting the option to request a transcript.

How long does it take to process a transcript request?

Processing time varies depending on the mode selected.

Can i track the status of my transcript?

Yes, you can track the status of your request within the app from the 'Order Tab'.

How will i be notified when my transcript is ready??

You'll receive a push notification informing you that your transcript is available for download.

How can i pay for my transcript?

You can securely pay for your transcript using MTN MOMO.
